How to make fluffy pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour or maida
  • 2 tbsp Sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • tsp Vanilla extract
  • 4 tbsp Melted butter
  • raspberry
  • sugar syrup

Preparation

  1. 1. In a small bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder and salt.
  2. 2. Combine egg, milk and shortening; stir into dry ingredients just until moistened.
  3. Pour batter by 1/4 cupfuls onto a greased hot griddle.
  4. Turn when bubbles form on top of pancakes.
  5. Cook until the second side is golden brown.
